Front-office-course

Our Front Office Course is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge required to excel in front office and customer service roles. It is a crucial part of the hospitality and service industry, and we take pride in preparing our students to be the best in this field.

Key topics covered in the Front Office Course include:

  • Customer Service Excellence
  • Front Desk Operations
  • Reservation Management
  • Communication and Interpersonal Skills
  • Time Management and Organization

Our course combines both theory and practical training to ensure our students are well-prepared for real-world challenges in front office roles. We emphasize problem-solving, effective communication, and creating a positive customer experience.

Key areas of study in our ICT program include:

  • Programming and Software Development
  • Network Administration
  • Web Development and Design
  • Cybersecurity and Data Protection

Tour Guide Operation Course

Our Tour Guide Operation Course is designed for individuals passionate about travel, exploration, and sharing knowledge with others. As a tour guide, you'll play a crucial role in providing unforgettable experiences to travelers and showcasing the beauty of different destinations.

Key topics covered in our Tour Guide Operation Course include:

  • Destination Knowledge and History
  • Cultural Sensitivity and Communication
  • Group Management and Safety
  • Tour Planning and Logistics
  • Interpretation and Storytelling

Our comprehensive program combines classroom learning with practical on-field training to ensure you acquire the necessary skills and expertise. Whether you dream of leading city tours, wildlife safaris, or cultural expeditions, our course will prepare you for a fulfilling career in the travel and tourism industry.
